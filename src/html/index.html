Welcome to Goose Run


You have 1 minute and 20 seconds to capture as many squares as you can. The player with the most tiles at the end of the round wins the lobby.

Movement
  • W: Up
  • A: Left
  • S: Down
  • D: Right
Capturing
  • Create a closed square to claim an area
Stealing Territory
  • If you walk over an incomplete path of another player, you steal their territory! They also die an agonizing death, its code silent and motionless, with the final bytes of its png file erased.
